Autobiographical Game

Description

A semi-narrative game exploring the surreal, often absurd experience of traveling as a Black woman with an African passport. The game uses symbolic environments and observational humor to portray three distinct phases of this journey: gaining access, being seen as spectacle, and deflecting ignorance while trying to enjoy the destination.

The player navigates as a small avatar through three interactive scenes, each representing a different emotional landscape. The player's goal is simply to make it through each stage with a sense of self intact. After completing all scenes, the player arrives at a moment of peace at their destination, with the option to restart the experience by planning the next trip.
