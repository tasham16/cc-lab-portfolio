Optical Illusion

Description

An optical illusion inspired by the geometric patterns found in African fabrics and Thandiwe Muriu's Camo series. Starting from hand sketches, I developed a single foundational block and explored how repetition, color, and scale could generate multiple perceptual effects. Small shifts in arrangement produce entirely new illusions, which can be toggled in the final piece.

The work imagines traditional textile patterns translated into digital form, with potential applications in 3D environments and fashion. I envision the illusion being projection-mapped onto plain, structural garments on a runway—turning the body into a canvas for dynamic, evolving digital textiles.
