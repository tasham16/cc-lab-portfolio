Data Portrait

Description

This project is a personal data portrait exploring how my music listening habits change across different daily activities. Over the course of a week, I tracked activity time blocks and mapped Spotify listening data to those sessions to identify genre patterns.

The visualization takes the form of an interactive guitar fretboard, where each string represents an activity and time unfolds horizontally. Listening sessions appear as segments along the strings, with genres shown as notes sized by frequency. Subtle animations like vibrating strings and glowing notes emphasize the relationship between music, time, and routine, highlighting how music helps structure my day and support motivation.
